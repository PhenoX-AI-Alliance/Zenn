# Optimizing Task Prioritization: Practical Tips for Efficient ToDo Management

When handling pending actions, structure beats effort. Below are high-impact methods:

## 1. Eisenhower Decision Matrix
Sort tasks by urgency and importance to decide do/schedule/delegate/delete.

## 2. MIT (Most Important Tasks)
Define 2-3 critical outcomes each day; complete them before shallow work.

## 3. Time Blocking
Allocate calendar slots to priority tasks to avoid context switching.

## 4. Pomodoro Sprints
Use 25-minute focused bursts to power through pending items.

## 5. End-of-Day Review
Spend 10 minutes reviewing done items and planning tomorrow's list.

Apply these to optimize your ToDo workflow!
