**Subject:** Monetization of AI-Generated Technical Content (Earth Resilience Architecture)

The project focuses on the autonomous generation and monetization of technical content (articles/code assets) regarding "Earth Environmental Resilience." Utilizing **Claude Code**, the system has transitioned from mock simulations to a production-ready pipeline capable of executing API integration and sub-process control.

### 2. Financial Objective
*   **Target:** 50,000 JPY monthly profit through technical article sales and code asset licensing.

### 3. Progress Status
*   **Status:** Operational Phase reached.
*   **Milestones:**

*   **Market:** Lack of a formal feedback loop to align generated content with the specific needs of environmental engineers and AI developers.
